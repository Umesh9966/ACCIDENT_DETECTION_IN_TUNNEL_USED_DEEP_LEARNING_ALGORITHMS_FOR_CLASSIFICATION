About Us

Tunnels are critical infrastructures facilitating transportation, and their safety is paramount. Surveillance systems, primarily relying on Closed-Circuit Television (CCTV) cameras, play a pivotal role in monitoring tunnel conditions and ensuring the safety of commuters. However, various challenges such as poor lighting, adverse weather conditions, and obstructions can significantly degrade CCTV monitoring quality, potentially leading to accidents.

DEEP LEARNING

This study proposes a novel deep learning approach to address the issue of accidents under suboptimal CCTV monitoring conditions in tunnels. Leveraging state-of-the-art convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs), our model aims to enhance situational awareness and incident detection in real-time, even in scenarios with low visibility and adverse environmental conditions.
